MINUTEZERO

Surviving the "Panic Gap" with Multimodal Intelligence

Executive Summary

In critical medical emergencies, the transition from trauma to treatment is plagued by a lethal void: The Panic Gap. This is the interval between an incident and the arrival of professional help, characterized by bystander fear, confusion, and delayed communication with hospital staff.

MinuteZero (MZ) is a zero-latency, multimodal AI agent that transforms any smartphone into a world-class ER specialist. By leveraging the Gemini 1.5 Pro Multimodal Live API, it sees, hears, and acts with authoritative guidance to save lives in the "Golden Minute."

👁️ The Problem: The Lethal Reach

Current emergency response relies on voice-only components, where untrained bystanders are forced to describe complex medical situations under extreme stress. This creates:

  • Latency of Description: Vital seconds lost explaining visual trauma.
  • Bystander Paralysis: Fear preventing critical interventions like CPR.
  • The Intake Log-jam: Hospitals receive victims with zero advance data.

Multimodal Real-Time Vision

MinuteZero processes interleaved tokens to identify specific clinical markers. It recognizes arterial bleeds from venous ones and detects pediatric airway obstructions through the lens—providing visually-aware instructions in real-time.

The Golden Minute Extension

By generating a Clinical Briefing during the intervention and syncing it via the Google Drive API, we ensure that specialized trauma bays and blood inventory are prepped before the patient arrives.

🛠️ Technical Engine

  • Core Engine: Gemini 1.5 Pro (Multimodal Live API) for interleaved reasoning.
  • Streaming: LiveKit Cloud for WebRTC orchestration.
  • Tools: Google Search for facility routing & Google Drive for data persistence.
  • Deployment: Google Cloud Run & Vercel.
