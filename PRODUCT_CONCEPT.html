Product Concept: "VitalSignal" (formerly MediGuide)

Core Value Proposition:
Closing the "Panic Gap"—the critical minutes between an emergency and the arrival of professional help.

2. Name Conflict Analysis

⚠️ Major Conflict Detected for "MediGuide"

Research confirms "MediGuide" is heavily used and actively trademarked in the medical space:

  • St. Jude Medical (Abbott): Uses "MediGuide Technology" for cardiac navigation systems.
  • MediGuide International: A global medical second-opinion service.
  • MediGuide App: Existing symptom checker on app stores.

Recommendation: Do NOT use "MediGuide". It confuses the brand and risks disqualification for unoriginality.
